PROJECTS

The work below details the main areas I have strong skills in. During my career I have worked on a variety of projects, including designing and coding emails, writing articles for StoneShot's blog and designing magazines, brochures and other key marketing materials. If there is something you'd like to know more on please feel free to contact me.

StoneShot collateral

I work closely with the Marketing Manager on projects such as the Digital Marketing Survey and UX/UI design for our marketing platform.

The Digital Marketing Survey involves a range of tasks, including designing and coding the email campaigns, coding the survey forms using ASP and JavaScript, post-survey analysis and designing the results brochures.

Other marketing collateral includes sales aids, email campaigns for prospects clients and folders.

View my project gallery here.

Email Marketing

In the past year I have greatly increased my knowledge in email marketing; not only do I amend and code emails for clients, I also create bespoke, responsive designs.

This is something I am passionate about and enjoy keeping up-to-date with the latest trends to ensure that all designs work across every email client and device.

Please get in touch if you'd like to see more examples of my work within email marketing.

View my project gallery here.

Magazine design

Whilst working with copywriters and the public relations team, I put together the company magazines, Prospects in Partnership and Prospects. This included the complete design, sourcing images or creating illustrations, as well as overseeing the print using local suppliers.

You can view the whole range here; I was responsible for issues 19 to 22 and Yorkshire and Humber, Issue 1.

View my project gallery here.

Prospects collateral

Producing corporate collateral for all parts of the business was a key responsiblity for me at Prospects. I was able to work on a variety of print material such as brochures, flyers, postcards and posters. I also looked after merchandising and arranged design and printing for stationary, pull up banners and event materials.

Working with such corporate branding allowed me to understand the importance of a company's brand and ensuring that all material came as part of a package.
